Coffee

Vietnam is proud to possess a rich cultural heritage of coffee, and 'Phin' is the soul, the culture of enjoying coffee that has been ingrained in the subconscious of many Vietnamese. Roasted coffee is extracted slowly drop by drop through a metal filter called 'Phin', as well as expressing the depth of each thought and sincerity in existing relationships. You can choose to drink it hot or serve it with ice, with or without condensed milk. Highlands Coffee is proud to serve Vietnamese coffee in the traditional Vietnamese way.

Cakes

Perhaps those who love cakes, especially those made in the French style, are no strangers to the Nguyen Son Bakery brand. Each cake at Nguyen Son Bakery has its own look, from taste to decoration. Simple form with only two main colors black and white, but quality thanks to the delicate and meticulous way. The cake has a sweet taste, not too bold, and the fat is smooth, so it does not cause boredom for people to enjoy. It is also very rare to find duplicates in cakes at Nguyen Son Bakery because all of them, from bread, cakes, cookies are 100% handmade. Moreover, the owner of the bakery, Chef Nguyen Son, is also quite picky in choosing ingredients for the shop's products.
